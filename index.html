Our Solution: The Manual Risk Audit

We provide the intelligence you need to move from a reactive to a proactive maintenance strategy. Our comprehensive on-site assessment is designed to identify your most critical vulnerabilities before they lead to failure.

🔍

On-Site Inspection

A thorough visual inspection of your main switchgear, critical panels, and major asset connections to identify obvious signs of wear, damage, or improper installation.

🌡

Thermal Imaging

We use non-invasive thermal cameras to identify hidden "hot spots" caused by loose connections or overloaded circuits—major precursors to fire and failure.

📜

Documentation Review

Analysis of your existing electrical schematics and maintenance logs to find systemic issues, outdated designs, and compliance gaps that could pose a risk.

📋

Actionable Risk Report

You receive a detailed 10-page report with photographic evidence, analysis, and a prioritized list of remediation steps to immediately improve your facility's reliability.
